About the Founder

Shubham Mishra is the founder of Chahat Organic, an initiative dedicated to promoting pure organic spices and chemical-free food in India. His work focuses on creating transparency in food sourcing while supporting farmers who practice natural farming methods.

About Chahat Organic

Chahat Organic is a food initiative that promotes natural and chemical-free products sourced directly from farmers. The goal is to create a farmer-to-family food system where people can trust the purity, quality and origin of the food they consume every day.

Founder Vision

“At Chahat Organic, our vision is to ensure that every household has access to pure, organic and chemical-free food. Food should come directly from farmers who respect nature and grow crops responsibly. Our mission is to build a transparent and trustworthy farmer-to-family food system where families can rely on the purity of what they eat.”

— Shubham Mishra Founder, Chahat Organic

Mission

The mission of Chahat Organic is to promote healthier lifestyles by encouraging natural food habits and supporting farmers who practice ethical and sustainable farming methods.

Farmer to Family Philosophy

The core philosophy behind Chahat Organic is to build a direct connection between farmers and families. By removing unnecessary middle layers, the initiative aims to deliver honest food that people can trust.
