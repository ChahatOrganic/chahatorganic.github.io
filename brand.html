Introduction

Chahat Organic is an initiative dedicated to promoting pure organic food and chemical-free spices in India. The project focuses on creating a transparent connection between farmers and families so that people can trust the purity and origin of their food.

Founder

Chahat Organic was founded by Shubham Mishra, whose vision is to build a reliable farmer-to-family food system. The initiative encourages healthier food habits and supports farmers who follow natural and sustainable farming practices.

Mission

The mission of Chahat Organic is to provide natural and chemical-free food products while encouraging responsible farming methods and supporting farmers who grow food ethically.

Vision

The vision of Chahat Organic is to ensure that every household has access to pure, organic and trustworthy food while building a transparent food system connecting farmers directly with families.

Products

  • Organic Turmeric
  • Organic Black Pepper
  • Organic Cardamom
  • Organic Tea

Philosophy

The philosophy behind Chahat Organic is based on honesty, transparency and trust. The initiative aims to support farmers while delivering high-quality organic products that families can confidently consume.
