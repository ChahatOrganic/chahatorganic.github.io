Chahat Organic was founded by Shubham Mishra with a simple yet powerful vision — to bring pure, natural and chemical-free food to families while building a transparent connection between farmers and households.

The Story Behind Chahat Organic

The name Chahat Organic represents a simple idea — a desire to bring pure, natural and trustworthy food to families. The initiative focuses on creating a transparent connection between farmers who grow food responsibly and households that value health and authenticity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote healthier lifestyles by delivering natural and chemical-free food products sourced directly from farmers who practice ethical and sustainable farming methods.

Our Vision

The vision of Chahat Organic is to build a transparent farmer-to-family food system where families can trust the quality, purity and origin of the food they consume every day.
